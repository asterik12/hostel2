News Topic

Hostel 2, our second home, is quite unlike any other experience you might've gathered in life. Students come here from different parts of Assam, even India. Unknown to each other, they start a new life here together and before you know it, they become a part of life, a family far from home. Starting out here might be a bit tough as the whole place and people are new to everyone. So it takes some time to adjust, naturally. But as soon as everyone gets comfortable with the whole situation, there's no going back. From then on starts a new chapter in the lives of the boarders of our beloved hostel. The four years spent here among friends, seniors and juniors alike are some of the best you've had. The whole atmosphere, the environment, the hostel life becomes inseparable even after graduating from here. Lastly, it is an experience required to be felt first hand, as it is unlike any other.

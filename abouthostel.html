About Hostel


Assam Engineering College was established on 1955 in Assam Engineering institute, Chandmari with only civil engineering department. After shifting the AEC campus in 1958 to jalukbari, Hostel-2 Assam engineering college was born. The hostel was inaugurated by the prime minister of India Jawaharlal Nehru. Since then Hostel-2 has given many good and best engineers and leaders to the state and the country who has been selflessly serving the nation and the state for its development. The alumnus of Hostel-2 has been spread across the world. The main motto of the hostel is “Attitude determines altitude”. It is situated just near by the college main building. One can reach college for their classes on foot by a little walk of 1-2 minute which serves as the main advantage of the students. The hostel has completed 60 years of its existence in 2018 i.e. the diamond jubilee. The superintended of the hostel is Dr Aroop Bardalai, Vice Principal Assam Engineering College. It hostel has many facilities which is being maintain by the boarders of the hostel. Some of them are- a 24 hour free wifi facilities and LAN facilities, it has a common room where one can watch the past glory and achievements of hostel-2 and as well as one can relax there by watching news, movies in the TV setup in the common room, Important meetings and planning for the hostel are done here. Besides studies, games and sports has been an integral part of student’s life, keeping that in view we have a volleyball court, badminton court, table tennis board, carom board, all the kits for playing cricket. One can go for playing cricket and football in the nearby College Pyro Field which is just (100-200)m away from the hostel. If someone is interested in playing lawn tennis and basketball one can go to the nearby basketball court and lawn tennis court which is just 2-3 min away from the hostel. We also have library facilities, subscription to various news paper and magazine which is being delivered to the hostel regularly. Staying fit is very important in student life, with this motive we also have a hostel gym equipped with good facilities. There is a spacious Dining-Hall which is maintained hygienically for hostel boarders to enjoy their meals; We have a cooperative mess that is managed by the boarders of the hostel. At the end of each month a ‘mess fest’ is organized every month.

We have clean and hygienic bathroom, toilets which is being clean on daily basis. Cleanliness is a very important aspects as without a clean niche one cannot concrete in their studies and their other work, and hence cleanliness is being maintain every day. The hostel is being clean everyday with daily workers employed for this job. In case someone wants to refresh their mind by the natural beauty- we also have a garden and a small pond type aquarium which is being given life in the winter seasons, one will be mesmerise by the beautiful flowers and the birds that comes in the garden during this time. AEC view point is also very near to Hostel-2, one can go there and enjoy the beautiful Deeporbil and the entire campus.
